Biography

My name is Julie Moquin. I was born in Quebec but grew up in Florida. I graduated from Rice University with a B.S. in Astrophysics, distinction in research, a Minor in Mathematics, and a Minor in French Studies. I am bilingual, with French being my native language. In my free time I like to coach/play tennis, embroider, play piano, and hike. My favorite hangout spot is the beach, whether it is rain or shine. I am eager to explore stellar populations, variability, and formation as well as exoplanet detection and demographics. I will be applying for graduate schools in Fall 2025 in pursuit of a PhD.
